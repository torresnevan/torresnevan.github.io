Building AI Literacy In The Classroom

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI Explained

AI, or artificial intelligence, is a technology that allows computers and machines to learn from data and make decisions similar to how humans do. It can recognize patterns, solve problems, and even improve its performance over time. Students interact with AI every day through tools like recommendations, chatbots, and smart assistants.

Understanding AI in Education

Key Concepts

Machine Learning

A type of AI where computers learn from data instead of being directly programmed.

Algorithm

A set of rules or steps a computer follows to solve a problem.

Chatbot / Virtual Assistant

Programs that talk or respond to users (Siri, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude).

AI Technologies in the Classroom

AI can be used across a variety of educational tools and resources. Today, some of the most popular AI services include ChatGPT, Quizlet, and Gemini. Each tool offers unique services that can help contribute to a student's academic journey. For students, AI can help provide a form of adaptive and personalized learning across a wide range of subjects. For teachers, AI can help with generating lesson plans, analyzing classroom data, and automating grading.
